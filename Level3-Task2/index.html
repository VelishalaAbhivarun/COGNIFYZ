Overview of the Internship Program

Cognifyz Technologies offers a Web Developer Internship designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry practices. This program is open to students and freshers from diverse backgrounds, providing a unique platform to gain hands-on experience in web development. The internship is available with flexible durations of 1,2 or 3 months to your schedule and learning pace.

Duration: 1,2 or 3 months.

Highlights & Benefits

  • Real-World Experience: Work on given projects and gain practical exposure to the latest web development tools and technologies.
  • Expert Mentorship: Receive one-on-one guidance from experienced professionals throughout your internship.
  • Flexible & Remote: Enjoy the convenience of remote work and a flexible schedule.
  • Structured Learning: Complete guided tasks and a capstone project to build your portfolio and website.
  • Career Growth: Top performers may be considered for full-time roles at Cognifyz Technologies.
  • Certification: Receive a certificate upon successful completion of the internship.
  • Stipend: Selected interns may be eligible for a performance-based stipend.
